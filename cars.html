Classic cars I could see myself owning

I love classic cars and am totally going to get one in the future, but as for most of the ones you still see on the roads, you're probably like "Wow that car's old, yet cool and nice" as opposed to "Wow I would love to have that car". Here's a small collection of some of the cars I've seen over the years that I love the look and/or feel of.

  • 1979 AMC Concord
  • 1998 Fox Body Mustang
  • 1975 AMC Gremlin X
  • 1st gen Ford Ranger
  • 1985 Pontiac Fiero
  • 1967 Ford Mustang Nightmare
  • 1st gen Mazda Miata
  • 1984 Nissan 300ZX
